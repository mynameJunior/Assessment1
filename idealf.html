The position of a Service Desk Support Analysts purpose is to provide technical support to users. According to the job requirements on the advertisement, the applicant must have relevant tertiary or certificate level qualifications, strong technical skills in Windows 10 based systems, have effective communication skills, a blue card, vaccinated, and adaptive skills in troubleshooting.
As a fledgeling within this workforce, my knowledge amounts to only using basic tools such as HMTL, CSS, GitHub, and a little of Notepad++. Despite using these useful tools my knowledge on them is awfully low.
While my current skills and knowledge may stray far from the essential requirements for this position, having a clear path in which I obtain them is needed. Such a path would include continuing this unit and further continuing studies within Bachelor of IT, find employment within an IT related workplace for experience, etc, and self-study using credible online educational materials/ resources.
